Deepfake Videos

Deepfake videos are artificially generated or manipulated videos that can make fake faces, voices, and expressions appear real. They can be used to spread false information, create fake evidence, damage reputations, and mislead people online.

As AI-generated content becomes more realistic, identifying fake videos has become important for digital safety, media trust, and online awareness.

Why Deepfake Detection Matters

AI is being used in many useful ways, but some people misuse it to create fake videos and spread negative narratives. Such videos can influence public opinion, mislead viewers, and reduce trust in digital media.

A deepfake detection system helps analyze suspicious videos and supports safer digital communication by identifying possible manipulation.
